About

I'm a wedding cryer. I talk to my dog- but who doesn't, really? I bake when I'm nervous, or happy, or sad, or really whenever. Because of said baking, I devote a lot of time to running, and am the proud owner of a 4:13 marathon PR. I've lived with Type 1 Diabetes for 21 years, and am an outspoken advocate and educator on diabetic issues. I drink way too much caffeine, stay up late to finish chapters, and have gotten up before the sun comes up since I was 14. Let's get to know each other, huh?
